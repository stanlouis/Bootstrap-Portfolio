About Me

How I got into Web Development

My first computer was the Apple Macintosh SE M5011. I hardly knew what to do with it at first, yet, my fascination with computers knew no bounds. I still remember the excitement and apprehension I felt after I built my first PC. I pressed the power button, waited and hoped that the computer would boot. Soon after I regularly read articles from howstuffworks.com - particularly anything related to the inner workings of a computer. In 2006 I received a Bachelors of science in computer science from Hawaii Pacific University. After watching from afar how Web Development was constantly evolving. I could not stay away for too long. I immerse myself studying all the different topics I could learn, all the different paths that are interconnected but vast enough that a focus choice was necessary.

I have started my journey toward becoming a full-stack web developer. Web Development has been changing at a very fast pace in past decade. As I look forward to learn and grow with the many innovations while mastering the fundamentals, one thing I know for sure is that I will never stop learning and will continue to be amazed by this exciting field.

Always learning and seeking new challenges

Current interests are Next.js, Gatsby, Meteor, React Native. Further down the road I plan to invest some time in machine learning with Javascript and Python.
